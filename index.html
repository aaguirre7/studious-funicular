About Me

LAURA SARNELLI UCELLI: 

A passionate fashion and style expert with over 20 years of industry experience.  My ability to understand and anticipate trends, along with my innate talent for creating unique and personalized looks, has allowed me to work with a wide range of clients and artists, from individuals looking to renew their wardrobes to film and television productions that need an expert eye for their costumes. My mission is to help each client express their true self through fashion, creating styles that not only look good but also tell a story through their attire.

About Me:

I am a fashion stylist and wardrobe creator dedicated to transforming the way people look and feel through fashion. With a background in character creation and years of experience in designing and customizing costumes, I have had the privilege of collaborating with Messi, Chayanne, Maluma, Ozuna, Alejandro Sanz, and Juanes in the music industry, as well as contributing to high-profile productions such as Puma, Adidas, Coca-Cola, Hyundai, AppleTV, Lifetime Movie Club, Cinemax and films.

I believe that fashion is an art form and a powerful tool for self-expression. I strive to understand the unique needs and desires of each client, offering stylistic solutions that reflect their personality, needs, and lifestyle. My approach is collaborative, creative, and always focused on highlighting the best in each person.

Specialties:

  • Key Costumer: Management and supervision of costumes for film and television productions, ensuring coherence and attention to detail in every outfit.
  • Fashion Stylist: Creation of innovative looks tailored to the latest trends, working with clients for photo shoots, events, and more.
  • Wardrobe Creator: Development of complete wardrobes that tell a story and reflect the personality of characters or individuals, from conceptual design to implementation.
